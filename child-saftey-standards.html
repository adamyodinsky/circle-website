Circle is committed to protecting children from child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and exploitation. To ensure the safety of all users, particularly minors, Circle implements the following measures:

1. Prohibited Content

Circle strictly prohibits:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Child exploitation or grooming content
  • Any content that endangers or harms minors

Violations result in immediate account suspension and reporting to authorities.

2. User Reporting Tools

Users can report harmful or suspicious behavior directly in-app through the "Report" feature found in user profiles and chat screens.

3. Content Moderation

Circle uses a combination of:

  • Automated moderation (flagging keywords/images)
  • Manual review by trained moderators

This helps detect and act on violations quickly.

4. Age Restrictions

Circle is not intended for users under the age of 18. Access for minors is prohibited and monitored.

5. Legal Compliance

Circle complies with:

  • Local and international child protection laws
  • Mandatory reporting laws for CSAM
  • Cooperation with law enforcement when needed
